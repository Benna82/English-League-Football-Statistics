All Premier League Club Payrolls for the 2022-2023 Season, including payroll allocation by positional groups. Premier League's Top 5 Highest Payrolls in 2022-2023 is led by Manchester United with an Estimated Gross Annual Payroll of £247,130,000 per year, or £4,752,500 per week, excluding bonuses.

  • Disclaimer

    Payroll is calculated as the sum of all estimated base salaries for a club in a given season, for a given term (Summer or Winter). This total does not included player bonuses or club staff. For this reason, payroll estimates are not intended to match a club's personnel costs in their annual accounts. Historical data is calculated using 'Combined' (Summer and Winter) rosters, meaning all unique players who played for the club in a given season are included in this calculation. As a result, historical payroll estimates may be inflated from actual spending. Historical coverage may be limited or incomplete.
